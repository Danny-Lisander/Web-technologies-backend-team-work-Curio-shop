I am 100% sure that every person reading this text has experienced a feeling of nostalgia. Each of us wanted to remember our childhood or youth by playing games, or listening to music on an old device, or even playing CDs on an old player. And sometimes it's not because you couldn't get through some level in a game on an old console, or you forgot the words to a song and decided to play a recording of it on the player. No, that's far from it, to put it mildly, because that game or that song can easily be found on the Internet in the public domain. What we need is the atmosphere of that carefree time. After all, agree, we no longer want to play that very game and become the best at it again, we want to play to recreate the very magical atmosphere that we had as children and from which we have only the warmest memories.
